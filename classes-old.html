Class Offerings

26 & 2

This is a heated class consisting of 26 classic Hatha yoga postures and two breathing exercises. The sequence is not connected with vinyasa; instead, focus is on breath and stillness in between postures. This class is perfect for all students, beginner through advanced. This series is extremely healing, detoxifying and balancing resulting in a feeling of deep calm.

Vinyasa (Hot or Warm)

This class consists of sun salutations, standing postures, forward folds, backbends and twists. Expect an introduction to a variety of arm balances, inversions and pranayama. Practitioners should be familiar with a basic vinyasa flow.

Beginner Vinyasa

This class is an introduction to vinyasa. It focuses on proper alignment in classic yoga postures. Beginner Vinyasa will introduce you to sun salutations, standing postures, forward folds, gentle backbends and gentle twists. This is the perfect way to start your yoga asana journey, no prior knowledge necessary!

Abbreviated Ashtanga Primary Series

This is a non heated, modified, and shortened version of the Ashtanga Primary Series. Each movement is synchronized with breath to heat the body internally and allow safe stretching. It is a practice that develops oneâ€™s psychological, spiritual and physical health. Focus will be on proper posture alignment, dristi and breathing.

Dynamic Flow

This style of yoga infuses dance, music and yoga asana. Dynamic Flow is designed to let you loosen your body, open your heart and calm your mind as you build heat through plyometrics, fluidly moving from pose to pose. This class is driven by music; you can be sure to dance, sweat, flow and have fun.

Restorative

This class consists of long holds and deep, passive stretching allowing you to slow down and open up throughout your body. During the longer holds of restorative yoga, your muscles will begin to deeply relax. You may use props, rather than your muscles at times, to support your body.

Hot Vinyasa with Yoga Nidra

This is a 90 minute version of our Hot Vinyasa class with the addition of Yoga Nidra, also known as yogic sleep. Yoga Nidra is a meditation and conscious relaxation practice that is intended to induce total physical, mental and emotional relaxation. Expect to leave this class rejuvenated.

Relax and Renew

This is a slower paced restorative class. The focus is on alignment and releasing muscle tension by incorporating longer holds. This class also includes tuning into the mind and body through meditation and a restoration. Relax and renew is bound to leave you feeling restored and rejuvenated.

Heated Hatha

This class uses the Bikram sequence as its backbone but has additional classic hatha poses, including standing postures, arm balances, backbends and inversions. There is minimal flow, focus is on stillness and breath within postures. This is a complete body workout, inside and out and will benefit anyone looking to advance their yoga practice. All practitioners welcome.
