Dana Fields

I have been practicing Hatha yoga since my early twenties. I visited studios and gyms from time to time but mainly practiced in my home until I discovered hot yoga and a warm community of yogis in 2013; I was instantly hooked!

I received my 200 Hour RYT certificate at Charm City Yoga in Baltimore, MD, now Yoga Works, under the guidance of Kim Manfredi in August of 2016. In July of 2018 I received an additional 300 Hour RYT certificate with international yoga teacher, Jared McCann, at Lighthouse Yoga School in Brooklyn, New York.

I practice yoga to find stillness and to cultivate loving kindness for myself and for others. Through my practice I draw a deeper connection to the self within my mind and body. My asana and meditation practice have allowed me to be a more patient, understanding and all around better human being.

I teach yoga because I believe in the power of it. I believe in caring for my mind and my body; carving out time to nourish myself allows me to cultivate compassion and care for others.
