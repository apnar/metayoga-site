Met·a

/ˈmedə/

Meta, short for metamorphosis; to transform; a change of the form or nature of a person into a completely different one.


A term, especially in art, used to characterize something that is characteristically self-referential; just like in yoga, the journey of the self, through the self, to the self.


About the thing itself; seeing the thing from a higher perspective instead of from within the thing; being self-aware; being the seer not the seen.
